callBack

In Javascript,a callback is a function passed as an argumnets to another function. The purpose of a callback is to be executed later,after the complietion of an asynchronous operation or a certain task.

callback hell,also known as the "pyramid of Doom" occurs when you have multiple nested callbacks,making the code hard to read and maintain.This often happens in asynchronous Javascript,especially when dealing with nested callBacks for I/O operation
